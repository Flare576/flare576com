# Things That Did Not Work

## Ethyl Acetate on PLA+

I tried soaking small pieces to form a "paste" that I could apply to other prints, and all I got was colored Highly Flammable liquid.

I tried rubbing a print with a microfiber cloth and Ethyl Acetate. It was great at discoloring the plastic and making a mess, not at smoothing or helping the print.
