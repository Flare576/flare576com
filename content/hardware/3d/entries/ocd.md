## Project Introduction

One problem with 3D printing is that the process leaves behind tell-tale layer lines.

![Layer Lines](/images/thumbnail/ocd_layer_lines.png)

So, when I got one, the first thing I decided to do was start putting together a toolkit for "smoothing" prints - the current state of the "Kit" is what you see above, and you might have noticed that the containers shapes and sizes are strangely _uniform_ for a guy who only started this hobby 3 months ago.

### Gridfinity

Way back in 2022, [Zack Freedman](https://www.youtube.com/c/ZackFreedman/about) revealed [Gridfinity](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ra_9zU-mnl8), an open (meaning anyone can print, sell, or extend), modular (you can mix/match pieces, stack, etc.) tool container system that you can [3D print](#/hardware/3d/what-is-3d-printing).

It's [grown a bit](https://github.com/jeffbarr/gridfinity-catalog) since then, and making your own containers is actually really straight forward. In a future post I'll try to walk you through the steps to making a basic bin!

#### Bento3D

Another option if you just want to make a tray to hold things is [Bento3D](https://bento3d.design/)!
